Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any further questions, please contact us and we will be happy to answer any questions you may have.

How does Ippocra save time?
Doctors are able to write, store and send reports all in one place, no need to use multiple devices or software. This reduces the complexity and time spent on writing reports.
Do I need a technical team to use Ippocra?
No, the platform is ready to use by any member of your staff. It doesn't require any technical expertize to write and send reports securely to your patients. Ippocra is made with doctors in mind, and we are constantly improving based on your feedback.
How does Ippocra increase patient retention?
We customize the report links with your logo and contact information. Patients that create accounts from your reports are also insentivized to become returning clients – returning clients get their Ippocra plans extended.
Is it difficult to integrate Ippocra with our management software?
No, Ippocra connects to the most commonly used systems without complex interventions. Integration with your managment system is simple and you will receive support on every step of the way from our technical team.
