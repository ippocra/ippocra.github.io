Frequently asked questions

If you have any other questions, contact us and we'll be happy to help.

Can I import reports I already have on USB keys or folders?
Yes. Ippocra's folder import lets you select an entire folder and upload all documents at once. The system analyses file names and folder structure to automatically assign each report to the right patient. You can review and adjust before confirming.
Is my patients' data safe?
Yes. All data is encrypted both at rest and in transit, stored in GDPR-compliant European infrastructure. We do not sell data to third parties or use it for advertising. You retain full control over your archive.
Can I access my archive from different clinics?
Yes. Your archive lives in the cloud and is accessible from any device with a browser — your laptop at home, the clinic PC, or even your phone. No USB keys to carry, no files to synchronise.
How is this different from an EMR?
EMR systems are tied to a single facility. If you work across multiple clinics, none of them gives you the full picture. Ippocra is your personal, portable patient archive that follows you everywhere — independent of any single clinic's system.
How much does it cost?
The Studio Trial gives you the full Studio Editor + DICOM experience for 30 days, no credit card required. After that, choose Studio, Studio Editor or Studio Editor + DICOM from the pricing page.
