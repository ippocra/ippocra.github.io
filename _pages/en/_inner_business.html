Frequently asked questions

If you have any other questions, contact us and we’ll be happy to answer any request.

How does Ippocra save time?
Doctors can write, store and send reports all in one place, without using multiple devices or software. This reduces complexity and the time spent managing reports.
Do I need a technical team to use Ippocra?
No, the platform is ready to use for any member of your staff. It requires no technical expertise to write and send reports securely. Ippocra is designed for doctors and continuously improved based on your feedback.
How does Ippocra increase patient loyalty?
We customize report links with your logo and contact information. Patients who create accounts from your reports are also incentivized to return: returning patients receive extensions of their Ippocra plans.
Is it hard to integrate Ippocra with our management software?
No, Ippocra integrates with the most commonly used systems without complex interventions. Integration with your software is straightforward and you’ll receive support at every stage from our technical team.
