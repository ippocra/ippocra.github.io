Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about using Ippocra to manage your health documents simply and securely.

What can I do with Ippocra?
You can upload reports, analyses, discharge letters and other health documents, keep them all in one place and find them instantly with smart search. You can also share them securely with your doctors via temporary links.
Are my health data really safe?
Yes. Your data is protected with advanced encryption both at rest and in transit and is handled in compliance with European personal data protection regulations. We do not sell your data to third parties nor use it for advertising.
How much does Ippocra cost?
You can start with a free plan, ideal for trying the service and organizing your first documents. When you need more storage or additional features, you can upgrade to the Pro plan at any time.
Can I use Ippocra for my family members' documents too?
Yes, that's exactly what it's designed for. Many users use Ippocra to manage health documents for children, parents or partners, so all important family information is collected and organized in one place.
How is it different from the Electronic Health Record or hospital apps?
The Electronic Health Record gathers some documents from public-sector systems. Ippocra lets you hold all your documentation: you can add reports from different facilities, private-practice documents, photos of paper reports and organize them however you like. You can do this for yourself and for your family members.
How does sharing with a doctor work?
You can create a temporary link that gives the doctor access only to the documents you choose, for the duration you set. It's a safer alternative to sending reports via email or chat. You can revoke access at any time.
Can I stop using Ippocra and delete my data?
Yes. You can stop using the service whenever you wish and request deletion of your data as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
