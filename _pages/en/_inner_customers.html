Features Designed for You

Smart Search

Navigate your health records with ease. Our smart search extracts content from within your documents, allowing you to find anything using any keyword, in any language your documents are written in!

Automatic Organization

Simply upload a health record – a PDF or even a photo from your phone. Ippocra will automatically extract the exam date, categorize the record, and digitize its text, making it instantly searchable with our Smart Search.

Safe with our Encryption

Your health records are exclusively yours. We employ state-of-the-art encryption to secure your data on disk, and we will never share it with anyone. Our subscription-based model ensures: You pay for the product, you are not the product.

IppoLink: Secure Sharing

Your health data is most valuable when you can securely share it with professionals. With IppoLink, you can easily create temporary, expiring links to share relevant medical history with new doctors. Avoid insecure methods like instant messaging – keep your data safe and share only when you want to.
