Frequently asked questions about Ippocra pricing

How much does Ippocra cost for a doctor?

The Studio plan costs €49 per month or €490 per year. It includes 1 admin, 1,000 patients, 2,000 document uploads per year, a patient timeline, multi-clinic cloud access and a 14-day free trial.

How much does Ippocra cost for users, families and caregivers?

Ippocra offers one free plan and three paid plans for users and caregivers: Just Us at €5/month or €50/year, Family at €8/month or €80/year, and Family Plus at €10/month or €100/year.

Are monthly and yearly prices always visible?

Yes. On this page, doctor pricing and family pricing are shown directly in the content, without an audience toggle. That makes the page easier to compare, read and index.

Is Ippocra useful when you need to retrieve and share reports quickly?

Yes. For doctors, it means finding patient history quickly across clinics. For families and caregivers, it means keeping an organised archive from which you can retrieve and share tests and reports without chasing scattered files.
