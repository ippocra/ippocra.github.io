## What is an ILAI?

An ILAI is not a SaaS tool you subscribe to. It's a complete artificial intelligence system that runs entirely on the company's own infrastructure — on-premise, behind the corporate firewall, under their control. No cloud dependencies, no data leaving the building, and no variable costs based on usage.

Each ILAI is customised for the specific business it serves. It learns the company's processes, documents, workflows, and culture. It becomes a digital colleague — not a chatbot, but an actual collaborator that grows more useful every day.

## Why local AI matters

The cloud AI model has one fundamental problem: **your data leaves your building**. Every query, every document processed, every piece of context is sent to a third party. For businesses that handle sensitive information — financial data, client records, strategic plans — that's not just inconvenient. It's unacceptable.

Local AI flips that equation:

- **Your data stays where it belongs.** Inside your company. Behind your firewall. Never transmitted.
- **Costs are fixed, not variable.** You know exactly what you're paying. No per-token surprise bills.
- **The AI becomes smarter over time.** Every interaction with your team adds to its knowledge base. It doesn't reset every session.
- **Full compliance.** GDPR, industry regulations, internal policies — everything is respected by design.

## From one to many

The first ILAI was a research effort — an experiment, a prototype, a demonstration created for internal use, because data matters and must always be preserved. And at Ippocra, we care about privacy. But ILAI could be useful to many people and companies, so we launched it as a product. And now we're scaling it.

Building multiple ILAIs simultaneously has taught us a lot:

- **Customisation is real, not theoretical.** Each ILAI needs to understand a different domain, different terminology, different workflows. We've built the framework to handle this efficiently.
- **Performance matters.** Local AI needs to be fast enough to feel instant, not a delayed response that kills the user experience.
- **Hardware selection is critical.** The right balance of compute, memory, and cost per deployment. We've optimised this.
- **Support is an ongoing relationship.** Every ILAI is a long-term partnership, not a one-off sale.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How is an ILAI different from ChatGPT or Claude?**

An ILAI runs on your own hardware, not on a cloud provider's servers. Your data never leaves your company. The AI adapts to your specific context instead of being a generic model. And your costs are fixed, not usage-based.

**Does an ILAI require an internet connection?**

No. The core AI runs entirely locally. An internet connection is only needed for updates and maintenance — not for daily operations or data processing.

**How much does an ILAI cost?**

The cost depends on your infrastructure needs, the scale of deployment, and the specific customisation required. Contact us for a personalised evaluation.

**Does ILAI replace people?**

No. ILAI is designed to be a collaborator, not a replacement. It works alongside your team, handles repetitive tasks, accelerates research and analysis, and becomes more valuable over time.

**Can I have my own ILAI?**

Absolutely. Every ILAI is customised for the specific business. If you'd like to see what an ILAI could do for your company, reach out to us at [info@ippocra.com](mailto:info@ippocra.com).
