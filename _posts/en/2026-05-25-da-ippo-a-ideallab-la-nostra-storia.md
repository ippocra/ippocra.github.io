Ippocra today has two sections: **Ippo**, the health platform for families, and **[Ideallab](https://ideallab.org)**, software and AI consulting for businesses.

But it hasn't always been this way.

---

## Ippocra was born with a single idea

It all started with **Ippo** — the health platform that organizes, finds, and securely shares medical records. We built it starting from a concrete problem we had seen up close: scattered documents, hard to find, impossible to share with anyone who isn't an expert.

The mission was clear: make health more manageable for Italian families. And for a while, that was all there was.

---

## The market spoke to us

While we were working on Ippo, requests from another direction began to come in more frequently. Businesses, small and medium-sized, were asking us: *"Can you build us a tool that does this?"*

We initially viewed them as distractions. Then we realized they weren't.

One of these projects became **[Rendiconto](https://rendiconto.ideallab.org/)** — born from direct experience with BRAL BRUSAFERRI SRL, a company that was manually cross-referencing invoices and transport tolls from different suppliers with different discounts. An incredibly long process, entirely manual, with no software able to solve it. We built it tailored to that specific case, and it worked.

But it was the feedback from others that changed everything: *"What you did for BRAL, can you do it for us too?"*

We realized we were facing a real, widespread need, and — most importantly — a need people were willing to pay for.

---

## The decision: opening Ideallab

We opened **[Ideallab](https://ideallab.org)**, the division dedicated to custom software and AI consulting for SMEs.

It wasn't in the initial plan. But it was the direction the market was forcing us toward.

Ideallab is born from a clear observation: **most SMEs don't need prefabricated tools — they have data problems, manual processes, and software that doesn't adapt to the way they work.** And those are the exact same skills we used to build Ippo: privacy-first, pragmatic, built around real-world workflows.

Today Ideallab does two things:

### Custom software

Tailored applications, designed around your workflows. Zero unnecessary features, zero compromises — just software that does what you need.

### AI consulting

Practical artificial intelligence integration for SMEs. We assess where AI generates value, recommend the right approach (often not the one you'd expect), and help you implement it.

And behind both lies the same promise: **your data stays yours**. No lock-in, no cloud API calls, no data leakage.

---

## Ippocra today: a company with two sections

After this discovery, we redefined what Ippocra is.

**Ippocra is the parent company** — a technology company building tools for the future of healthcare and business. It has two sections:

- **Ippo** — the health platform for families and patients
- **[Ideallab](https://ideallab.org)** — custom software and AI for SMEs

Two sections, one company. Two complementary missions, one common approach: building software that adapts to people, not the other way around.

---

## Where we're putting our energy

Let's be honest: today we're putting everything into Ideallab.

Ippo isn't on pause. It's a working product, we're improving it day by day, and we'll continue to serve it. But the reality is the market is speaking loudly, and Ideallab is where we're concentrating our energy.

Ippo **isn't going anywhere**. It will remain active, continue to receive maintenance and new improvements, and will continue to serve those who already use it and those who will in the future. But the main growth and development are on Ideallab.

This is the life of a startup: discover where the market needs you most, and go there. It's not a rethink — it's an evolution.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Ideallab a spin-off from Ippocra?**

No. Ideallab is not a spin-off or a separate company. It's a division of Ippocra S.R.L. — like Ippo, it's part of the same company. We have two sections operating under the same roof, with the same team and the same principles.

**Will Ippo continue to be developed?**

Yes. Ippo will continue to receive maintenance, new improvements, and support for all current and future users. It's not a suspended project — it's simply the division we're not actively pushing forward at the moment.

**What is local AI?**

Local AI means running artificial intelligence models directly on your infrastructure — your server, your computer, your network. No data leaves your control. There are no calls to external cloud APIs, no lock-in with suppliers, no risk of data leakage. It's AI that works like a tool in your company, not a service that uses your data.
