Trade associations are a bridge between technology and the territory they serve. When an organization like Confartigianato Ancona Pesaro Urbino chooses to act as a conduit for introducing new digital tools among its member businesses, it's not just offering a service — it's building a model of shared innovation.

This is the direction Ippocra and Confartigianato are walking together, through a partnership for the spread of local artificial intelligence via [**ILAI**](https://ilai.ippocra.com/en/) — the AI system that lives on the user's own infrastructure, with their data, under their control.

---

## The role of associations in digital innovation

In Italy, trade associations represent a fundamental part of the economy. Confartigianato Ancona Pesaro Urbino, in particular, has a network of hundreds of craft businesses that every day manage quotes, invoices, projects, and client documents — sensitive data that cannot and should not leave the company.

The leap toward digitalization is never straightforward for these realities. It's not just about buying software or subscribing to a cloud service. It's about finding tools that respect existing processes, don't put company data at risk, and have costs that businesses can predict and control.

This is where the partnership between Ippocra and Confartigianato finds its purpose: not as a product sold to a customer, but as a journey built together, where the association acts as a value multiplier for all the businesses it represents.

---

## Why local AI

The choice to focus on local artificial intelligence comes from a clear observation: craft businesses — like many SMEs — handle information that is their business asset. Pricing, margins, customer relationships, ongoing projects. This information must stay within the company.

A cloud-based AI, by its very nature, requires data to leave the user's infrastructure. A local AI, on the other hand, runs on the user's machine. It doesn't call external servers. It doesn't send anything to third parties. It works — in many cases — even without an internet connection.

For an association that needs to evaluate a tool to propose to its businesses, this is a decisive factor: it's not recommending something that exposes its members to privacy risks or lock-in with external providers.

---

## The partnership model

This collaboration with Confartigianato follows an approach we can describe as "network-based":

- **The association** identifies the needs of its territory and its member businesses
- **Ippocra** brings the ILAI technology, the experience in local deployment, and technical support
- **Member businesses** gain access to a tool that can transform how they work, without risking their data

The advantage for the association is clear: it offers tangible value to its members, demonstrating that innovation isn't only for large companies, but something that can enter the workshop, the professional office, the factory floor.

The advantage for Ippocra is equally clear: working through associations means reaching businesses that otherwise wouldn't have access to these technologies, and doing so with the benefit of the trust an association has already built in its territory.

---

## Control, privacy, predictable costs

For a craft business, cost certainty is essential. You can't manage a business with variable costs that depend on how much you use a cloud service, how many APIs you call, how much "intelligence" you consume.

With ILAI, costs are fixed and predictable. The AI runs locally. There are no usage-based fees, no surprises on the invoice. The company knows exactly what it's spending and why.

And the data? It stays exactly where it has always lived: within the company's own infrastructure. No third-party cloud, no external APIs, no data leakage. Control is total — and this applies both to the individual business and to the association that wants to guarantee its members a level of data protection it can truly stand behind.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is ILAI?**

ILAI is artificial intelligence that runs locally on your own infrastructure. It's not a cloud service, it doesn't require internet connectivity for processing, and it never sends your data to external servers. It's a "digital colleague" that works inside your business, with your data, under your control. It's pronounced as a single word, not individual letters.

**What kind of partnership is this between Ippocra and Confartigianato?**

It's a collaboration where Ippocra provides the ILAI technology and technical support, while Confartigianato Ancona Pesaro Urbino acts as the bridge to member businesses in its territory. It's not a traditional sale — it's a journey built together, with the goal of bringing practical digital tools to those who need them most.

**Why are associations the right channel for local AI?**

Because associations already have the trust of businesses. They know their needs, their processes, their challenges. Offering a tool through a trusted channel makes innovation less abstract and more accessible.

**Does ILAI replace people?**

No. ILAI is designed to be a collaborator, not a replacement. It works alongside the team, automating repetitive tasks, helping with research and analysis, and becoming more useful over time as it learns the context.
