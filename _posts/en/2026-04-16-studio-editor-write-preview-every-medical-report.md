## What Is the Studio Editor?

The Studio Editor is Ippocra's built-in professional report-writing tool. It replaces the basic editor we [launched when we first introduced report writing inside Ippocra](/write-reports-in-a-breeze) with a far more capable environment designed to match the workflow of a busy specialist.

Whether you see five patients a day or fifty, the Studio Editor adapts to your pace:

- **Rich-text formatting** — headings, bold, italic, bullet and numbered lists, undo/redo, all in a clean writing surface.
- **Live split-screen PDF preview** — toggle a side-by-side view to see exactly how the finished document will look before you save or send it.
- **Automatic draft recovery** — if your browser closes unexpectedly, your unsaved work is restored the next time you open the editor.
- **DICOM attachment** — attach diagnostic imaging studies directly to the report and share everything with the patient in one step via IppoLink.

## Security, Compliance and IppoLink Sharing

Every document created in the Studio Editor is stored encrypted (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit) in GDPR-compliant European infrastructure. This is not an optional extra — [the law requires strict archiving standards for medical reports](/medical-reports-how-professionals-should-store-them), and Ippocra is built to meet them from the ground up.

Once a report is saved, you can share it with the patient instantly via an IppoLink — a secure, time-limited link that does not require the patient to create an account. The patient can view and download their document from any device.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the Studio Editor work on mobile?**
Yes. Ippocra is a progressive web app that runs on any modern browser — desktop, tablet, and mobile. The editor is fully responsive, though for extended writing sessions a laptop or desktop is recommended.

**Can I attach images and diagnostic files to a report?**
Yes. With the Studio Editor + DICOM plan you can attach imaging studies directly to the report. Patients receive the full document and the imaging data in a single, secure IppoLink.

**Is there a limit on how many reports I can write?**
The Studio Editor plans include 2,000 documents per year. If your practice produces more, contact us at [info@ippocra.com](mailto:info@ippocra.com) and we'll find the right solution for you.

**Can I migrate my existing reports to Ippocra?**
Yes. Use Ippocra's folder import feature to upload your existing archive in one go. The system analyses file names and folder structure to assign each document to the right patient automatically.

**How does the letterhead customisation work?**
From inside the editor, open the Theme Manager, upload your clinic logo (PNG or SVG), choose your accent colour and font, and set the header and footer text. Save the theme and it is applied to every new report automatically.
