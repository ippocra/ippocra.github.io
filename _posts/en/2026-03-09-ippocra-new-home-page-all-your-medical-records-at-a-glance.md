## What's New on the Ippocra Home Page

The updated home page is built around two fundamental questions: **who** and **when**. At the top of the screen you see every member of your family — or everyone included in your plan — each with an indicator showing how many documents they have and when the last upload happened. One tap is all it takes to switch to that person's timeline.

- **All family members** registered in your account, with their profile picture or initials.

Whether you manage records for an elderly parent, a child, or a partner, the overview screen removes the cognitive overhead of switching contexts — everything is visible at a glance.

## A Timeline of All Your Uploaded Documents

Below the people row, a **chronological timeline** lists every document uploaded within your family group. The most recent items appear first. Each entry shows the document type, the person it belongs to, and the upload date — finding the right file takes seconds rather than minutes.

The timeline supports multiple document categories:

- **Lab results** — blood panels, urine tests, cultures
- **Imaging** — X-rays, MRI, CT scans, ultrasounds, echocardiograms — [all viewable directly in the browser](/how-to-view-your-medical-imaging-records-directly-online)
- **Prescriptions** — medications, specialist referrals
- **Specialist reports** — cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, and more

Documents are colour-coded by category, so a quick glance tells you what type of record you're looking at.

## Why a Unified Health Dashboard Matters

Most people store health documents in a mix of physical folders, email attachments, phone photos, and cloud drives. When a doctor asks "do you have your last blood test from six months ago?", the answer is often a frustrated search across three different apps. Ippocra's home page solves exactly this problem.

A unified health dashboard means:

- **Faster appointments** — walk in with every relevant document already at your fingertips.
- **Better continuity of care** — specialists see the full picture, not just the documents you happened to bring.
- **Peace of mind for caregivers** — if you manage someone else's health, you see the same complete timeline they do.
- **No lost documents** — once uploaded, a record is always there, always searchable, never misplaced. Already have a folder full of existing records? [Import the whole folder in one go](/import-a-whole-folder-and-auto-assign-documents-to-the-right-person) and Ippocra assigns each document to the right person automatically.

Privacy is built in from the start. Every document is encrypted at rest and in transit, and you control exactly who can see each record.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I see documents for multiple family members on the same screen?**
Yes. The home page shows all family members in a row at the top and the full timeline beneath them. You can filter the timeline to a single person or view everyone's records together.

**What types of documents can I upload to Ippocra?**
You can upload any health-related document — lab results, imaging reports (including DICOM files), prescriptions, vaccination records, and specialist letters. PDF, JPEG, and PNG formats are all supported.

**Is my medical data private and secure?**
All documents are encrypted with AES-256 at rest and transmitted over TLS 1.3. You decide who has access to your records; no document is shared without your explicit permission.

**Can I use Ippocra on my phone?**
Ippocra is a progressive web app that works on any modern browser — iOS, Android, and desktop — with no app installation required. The home page is fully responsive and works just as well on a phone as on a laptop.
