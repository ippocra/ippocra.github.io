Ippocra has taken another important step toward making local artificial intelligence accessible: a **framework agreement with Confartigianato Ancona Pesaro Urbino** for the distribution of [**ILAI**](https://ilai.ippocra.com/en/) — the local artificial intelligence that runs on your own infrastructure.

This partnership opens the door for hundreds of local craft businesses to discover a tool that isn't a cloud service, but a **digital colleague** working inside your company, with your data, under your control.

---

## Why this agreement matters

A framework agreement is not a routine commercial arrangement. It is an **official recognition** of three fundamental things:

1. **The strength of the ILAI proposition.** The associations have evaluated our solution and recognized that it addresses a real need — an artificial intelligence that helps businesses without putting their data at risk.

2. **The awareness of the privacy need.** Local artisans and small businesses know that their data is their most valuable asset. This framework agreement confirms that local AI is no longer an option for a few tech-savvy users, but a **business necessity** for any company handling sensitive information.

3. **Full user control.** With ILAI, data stays exactly where it has always been: inside the company's own infrastructure. No third-party cloud servers, no external API calls, no data leakage. The system is under complete user control — no lock-in, no hidden variable costs.

---

## The security that business demands

For a craft business, cybersecurity is not a luxury. Every day, they manage quotes, invoices, client documents, and technical projects — information that must never leave the company.

ILAI addresses this need with a **privacy-first approach by design**: artificial intelligence runs locally on the user's machine, without requiring internet connectivity for primary processing. This means:

- **Your data never leaves your business.** There are no API calls to external servers.
- **No risk of data breaches.** Sensitive data stays within your infrastructure.
- **Predictable, controlled costs.** You know what you're spending, when, and you don't depend on third-party usage-based pricing.
- **A colleague that grows with you.** ILAI adapts to your processes, learns from your context, and becomes a more valuable asset over time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is ILAI?**

ILAI is artificial intelligence that runs locally on your own infrastructure. It's not a cloud service, it doesn't require internet connectivity for processing, and it never sends your data to external servers. It's a "digital colleague" that works inside your business, with your data, under your control. It's pronounced as a single word, not individual letters.

**What is a framework agreement?**

A framework agreement is a contract that establishes the general conditions for future commercial operations. In this case, it defines the scope, terms, and methods by which ILAI will be offered and implemented for businesses associated with the two territorial associations.

**Why is local AI important for craft businesses?**

Craft businesses manage sensitive documents daily — quotes, invoices, projects, client data. With local AI, these stay inside your company. No cloud, no external APIs, no risk of data leakage. In addition, costs are predictable and don't depend on third-party usage-based pricing.

**Does ILAI replace people?**

No. ILAI is designed to be a **collaborator**, not a replacement. It works alongside your team, automating repetitive tasks, helping with document research and analysis, and becoming more useful over time as it learns your context.
